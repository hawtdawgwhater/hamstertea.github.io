About

Welcome to our whimsical boba shop, where every sip is an adventure waiting to be discovered! At our shop, we believe that bubble tea should be more than just a drink; it should be an experience that delights all your senses. Our menu features an array of flavors, from classic milk teas to creative fruit-infused blends, each crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure freshness and quality. One of our standout features is our signature tapioca pearls, which are playfully inspired by the enchanting sparkle of hamster eyes. These delightful pearls offer a unique texture and a hint of fun in every sip, making your boba experience truly one-of-a-kind. We take pride in experimenting with new flavors and textures, so you can always expect something exciting on your next visit. Our cozy and inviting atmosphere is perfect for catching up with friends, studying, or simply enjoying a moment of indulgence. Whether you’re a boba enthusiast or trying it for the first time, we’re here to make your visit unforgettable. So come on in, explore our playful menu, and discover the magic in every cup!
